What is Antenatal Care?

Antenatal care refers to the regular medical checkups and monitoring provided during pregnancy to ensure the health of both mother and baby.

  • ✔ Regular health assessments
  • ✔ Trimester-wise monitoring
  • ✔ Blood tests & medical evaluations
  • ✔ Prescription management
  • ✔ Risk assessment & early detection
